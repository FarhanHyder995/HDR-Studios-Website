Neversong

Neversong is a haunting 2D action-adventure game. You play as Peet, a teenager who wakes from a coma to find his town deserted of adults and his friend Wren missing. As he ventures through a surreal world filled with strange creatures, abandoned places and dark mysteries, Peet must rescue Wren, uncover the truth of his coma and survive the nightmare-ish depths of Blackfork Asylum.

Pinstripe

Pinstripe is a beautiful dark adventure-platformer created by Thomas Brush. You play as Teddy, an ex-minister who journeys through a frozen afterlife in search of his kidnapped daughter, taken by a sinister figure known as Pinstripe. The game combines hauntingly atmospheric visuals, emotional storytelling, and a melancholy soundtrack.
